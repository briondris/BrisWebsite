    • About
    Today I'am an engineer at BrightLine Interactive. I gained experience as a full-stack developer working at an array of companies, start ups, and non profits. My professional experience has allowed me to gain a variety of skills in full-stack development, VR and AR technologies, visual communication, and collaboration. I know that my passion for work is achieved when I translate between the program and the designed experience.
    I create interactive experiences that invite you to play at the edges of the unknown. I am driven by a deep curiosity to find the unseen choreographed patterns that make up the fantastic rituals in the everyday world. In my work, I explore digital puppets, femininity, and cyborgs. My personal and curated pieces continue to push the interaction between digital space and our own to discover territory where something human can unfold between the two worlds.
