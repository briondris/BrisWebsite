    • About
    In a past life, I worked solely as a full-stack developer for a nonprofit start-up to an insurance corporation. I found that the most joyful parts of my work were when I was developing and taking risks to include better UI and UX in the projects. Through my experiences I have learned an overarching variety of skills, but most importantly that my place in the working world is to be the translation between the functionality a program and the art and design of how it is to be used.
    I create interactive experiences that invite you to play at the edges of the unknown. I am driven by a deep curiosity to find the unseen choreographed patterns that make up the fantastic rituals in the everyday world. In my work, I explore digital puppets, feminity, and cyborgs. In the work I make and have curated, I continue to push the interaction between digital space and our own, to discover territory where something truly human can unfold between the two worlds.
